GENE DEICH

"The man who made magical of our childhood life"




Timeline of Gene Deich's Life:


  • 1924 - Birth Date: Gene Deitch was born in Chicago on 8thAugust.


  • 1929 - Teenager Years: The family moved to California, and Deitch attended school in Hollywood.


  • 1942 - Starting Career: Gene Deitch graduated from Los Angeles High School.


  • 1943 - Marriage: Deitch met his first wife, Marie, when they both worked at North American Aviation, and they married.


  • 1950-1951 - First recording: Deitch contributed covers and interior art to the jazz magazine The Record Changer. Deitch was an early supporter and audio engineer for Connie Converse, one of the first American singer-songwriters, recording sessions.


  • 1955 - Gene Deitch's creativity: Deitch was known for creating animated cartoons such as Munro, Tom Terrific, Nudnik, Popeye and Tom and Jerry series.


  • 1956 - Supervising: Gene Deitch took his talents to the Terrytoons studio, where he became supervising director.


  • 1960 - Academy Award: Gene Deitch won an Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film.


  • 2003 - Winsor McCay Award: Deitch was awarded the Annie Awards' Winsor McCay Award by ASIFA-Hollywood for a lifetime contribution to the art of animation.


  • 2016 - Deitch's collection: Gene Deitch who curated Tom and Jerry: "The Gene Deitch Collection" for Warner Home Video.


  • 2020 - Death: Deitch died in Prague on April 16, at the age of 95.
